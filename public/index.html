How Myngenda Works

Getting your package delivered is as easy as following these simple steps

1

Request a Delivery

Enter your pickup and delivery locations, package details, and preferred delivery time through our app or website.

2

Get Matched with a Driver

Our system matches your delivery request with a nearby verified driver who will pick up your package.

3

Track Your Delivery

Monitor your package's journey in real-time on the map and receive updates at each stage of the delivery process.

4

Secure Delivery Confirmation

Recipient confirms delivery through the app, ensuring your package has been safely delivered to the right person.
